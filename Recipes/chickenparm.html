Ingredients

  • 1lb Chicken Breast
  • Italian breadcrumbs
  • All Purpose Flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Olive oil
  • 28oz can crushed tomato
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Butterly the chicken
  2. Dip the chicken in the flour
  3. Dip the chicken in the beaten egg
  4. Dip the chicken in the breadcrumbs
  5. Pan fry the chicken in the oil until golden brown on both sides
  6. Top with cheese
  7. Place in broiler on Hi for 5 minutes or until cheese is browned
  8. Top with tomato sauce
