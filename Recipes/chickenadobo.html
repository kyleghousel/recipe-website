Filipino Chicken Adobo


Take Me Home

"Destination ohhh no chicken chicken chicken adobo" - Rancid

Description


When I was just a boy in the mountain ranges of Bavaria, my Nonna would yell
"Warum siehst du aus wie ein frosch?" What a character she was. Anyway here's a recipe
for Chicken Adobo, my favorite Filipino dish.

Ingredients

  • 3lb Chicken drums
  • 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/3 Cup white vinegar
  • 1 scallion
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbps peanut oi.
  • 4 Bay Leaves
  • 1 Tbsp whole peppercorn

Steps

  1. marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, bay leaves, and garlic
  2. Brown chicken on both sides, set aside
  3. Pour marinade into hot dutch oven, bring to simmer
  4. Put chicken back in, add peppercorn
  5. Simmer for 25 minutes, rotating chicken halfway
  6. Serve right away with rice
