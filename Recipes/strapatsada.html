Strapatsada (Greek scrambled eggs with tomato and feta)


Take Me Home

Scrambled eggs. Fresh tomato. Cheese. What else do you need?

Once upon a time in America, there was a boy. Maybe it was me. It was me. I'm the main character.
One day he had eggs for breakfast and he thought to himself "Wow I wish I had the Greek
version! Here is that version little me. This is the recipe. Coming up right after this paragraph.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 1 tbsp feta cheese
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs
  2. Saute tomato on hot skillet with olive oil, add salt and pepper
  3. Whisk in eggs, whisky constantly
  4. Top with feta
